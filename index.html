Mohit Agrawal is a Ph.D. candidate in economics at Yale University and is an applied microeconomist.

My research interests involve studying the impact of public programs on human capital development. I am particularly interested in applications in education and health.

I work with Jason Abaluck, Joe Altonji and Costas Meghir at Yale. I am also affiliated with the Institute for Social and Policy Studies at Yale and the Becker Friedman Institute at the University of Chicago.
