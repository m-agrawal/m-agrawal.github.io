Mohit Agrawal is a Ph.D. candidate in economics at Yale University and studies applied microeconomics.

My research interests involve applications of peer effects, networks, and information to different policy-relevant contexts. I am particularly interested in applications in education, health economics, and political economy.

I work with Jason Abaluck, Joe Altonji, Steve Berry, Yusuke Narita, and Ebonya Washington at Yale. I am also affiliated with the Institute for Social and Policy Studies.
