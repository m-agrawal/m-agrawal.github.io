Mohit Agrawal is a Ph.D. candidate in economics at Yale University and is a affiliated to the Institute for Social and Policy Studies.

I am a public and labor economist and am advised by Joe Altonji, Costas Meghir, Steve Berry, and Jason Abaluck. My research interests include the economics of education, health economics, and political economy. I approach my research using a variety of tools, including structural techniques, network econometrics, machine learning, and Bayesian statistics.
