Research Interests

My active research includes projects on (1) information costs and school choice, (2) medical technology diffusion, and (3) sources of political polarization.


Work-in-Progress

My current work-in-progress includes an extension of the Altonji-Mansfield model for selection on unobservables to separately identify the impact of location effects from school effects (joint with Altonji and Mansfield). Draft of working paper is available upon request.
